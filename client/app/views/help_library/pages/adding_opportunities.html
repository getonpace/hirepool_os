Adding Opportunities

An opportunity in hirepool is anything that you are pursuing as a job. You'll store opportunities in hirepool when you find them on company job sites, big job boards like Monster and Indeed, or when you generate them by networking.

There are a few different ways to add opportunities in hirepool. You can add them with the Add New button in the header, via CSV upload, or by using the hirepool chrome extension.
