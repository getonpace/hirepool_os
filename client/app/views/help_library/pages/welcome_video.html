Here's a quick overview of hirepool:

Thanks for joining hirepool! The job search can be a stressful and frustrating process. Hirepool is 100% designed for the job seeker and dedicated to improving the job seeker experience one interview at a time.

How does hirepool work?

  • Keep all of your job search in one place.
  • Receive tailored interview preparation and feedback.
  • Get personalized guidance throughout the offer process.
