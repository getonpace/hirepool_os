Collaborator Experience

What does a collaborator see when you invite them to help with your job search and give feedback on opportunities?

First they get an email from hirepool support:

Email in the collaborator's inbox

The full email the collaborator gets

By clicking the here link the collaborator is taken to a view in hirepool where they can give feedback on your opportunity:

The collaborator feedback page

This page doesn't require the collaborator to signup, login, or have a hirepool account in any way. It is a single use authentication page that can only be accessed via the url sent in the collaborator email.

And that's it! That's how we collect collaborator feedback in hirepool. After your collaborator submits feedback we'll send you an email to let you know.
